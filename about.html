The full story

I'm a Data Analyst with a background in Applied Mathematics (M.Sc., HSE Moscow) and Theoretical Physics (B.Sc., UNN). I bring a scientific approach to every problem — understand the system, build carefully, verify the results.

At Yandex I own product performance analytics for a browser search product serving over 100 million daily active users. Before that I was at X5 Retail Group building analytics for one of Russia's largest retail chains.

Outside of data work, I build things. My most involved project to date is a tube guitar amplifier — designed from scratch with an original schematic, 3D-modelled chassis, and entirely hand-wired. It took months and taught me more about electronics than any course could.

I also play guitar and write my own compositions. There's something satisfying about the full loop: write the music, build the amp, play it through the amp you built.

I'm currently based in Almaty, Kazakhstan, and open to relocation — EU Blue Card eligible. I speak English at C1 level and am actively learning German (B1).
